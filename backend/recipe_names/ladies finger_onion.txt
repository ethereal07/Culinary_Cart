1. Bhindi Masala: This is a popular Indian dish where ladies finger (also known as okra or bhindi) is cooked with onions and a blend of spices. It is usually served with roti or rice.

2. Okra and Onion Stir Fry: This is a simple and healthy dish where ladies finger and onions are stir-fried with garlic, soy sauce, and other seasonings. It can be served as a side dish or with rice.

3. Okra and Onion Soup: This is a comforting soup where ladies finger and onions are cooked with chicken or vegetable broth, tomatoes, and spices. It can be served with bread or as a light meal on its own.
