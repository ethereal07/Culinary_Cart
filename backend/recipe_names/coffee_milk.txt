1. Coffee Panna Cotta: This is a creamy, coffee-flavored Italian dessert made with coffee, milk, sugar, and gelatin. It's often served with a drizzle of chocolate sauce or a sprinkle of cocoa powder.

2. Coffee Milkshake: A refreshing and energizing drink made by blending coffee, milk, ice cream, and sugar. You can top it with whipped cream and a sprinkle of cocoa powder for extra indulgence.

3. Coffee Tiramisu: A popular Italian dessert that layers coffee-soaked ladyfingers with a creamy mixture of mascarpone cheese and sugar, all dusted with a generous amount of cocoa powder. The coffee and milk are used to soak the ladyfingers, giving them a rich, robust flavor.
