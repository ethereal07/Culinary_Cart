1. Paneer Tikka: This is a popular Indian dish where paneer (cottage cheese) is marinated in spices and then grilled or baked. Tomatoes are used in the marination process and also grilled along with paneer.

2. Paneer Butter Masala: This is a rich and creamy dish where paneer is cooked in a tomato-based gravy with lots of butter and cream. It is usually served with naan or rice.

3. Paneer Bhurji: This is a scrambled paneer dish cooked with tomatoes, onions, and various spices. It is a popular breakfast dish and can be served with bread or roti.
