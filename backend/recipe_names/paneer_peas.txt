1. Matar Paneer: This is a popular North Indian dish made with paneer and peas cooked in a tomato-based sauce. It is usually served with rice or roti.

2. Paneer Peas Pulao: This is a flavorful rice dish made with paneer, peas, and a variety of spices. It can be served with raita or a side salad.

3. Paneer Peas Paratha: This is a stuffed flatbread where the stuffing is made from crumbled paneer and peas. It is typically served with yogurt or pickle.
