1. Stuffed Capsicum with Mashed Potatoes: This meal involves hollowing out capsicums and filling them with a delicious mixture of mashed potatoes, cheese, and spices, then baking until the capsicum is tender and the filling is golden brown.

2. Potato and Capsicum Stir Fry: This is a simple, healthy dish where potatoes and capsicum are stir-fried with garlic, onions, and a variety of spices. It can be served as a side dish or as a main course with rice or bread.

3. Potato and Capsicum Curry: This is a hearty, flavorful dish where potatoes and capsicum are cooked in a rich, spicy curry sauce. It's perfect for serving with rice or naan bread.
